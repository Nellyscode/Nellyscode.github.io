Meet Author Nelly Nuñez

Nelly is a childrens's book author born and raised in Los Angeles. She earned a B.A in Child and Adolescent Developement and spent years working in early childhood education, where she fell in love with picture books. Now living in Salt Lake City with her husband and daughters, she writes heartfelt pictures books that support social-emotional learning. Nelly is represented by Lissa Woodson at The Macro Group.
